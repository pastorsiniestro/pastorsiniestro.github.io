Invitation

Sinister Church is a private community where new members are accepted by invitation only. Invitations are issued by those who already share our vision and believe the other person would benefit from our teachings. Our church members come from all walks of life and represent diverse lifestyles. Reach out to your family and friends and ask them if they are part of our community, if they are, request an invitation from them when you feel ready to join. At the present time this is the only way to join our comunity.
